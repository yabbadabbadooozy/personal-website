Anthony Ricco, MD

Radiation oncologist dedicated to Main Line Health, pairing stereotactic expertise with compassionate guidance so every patient understands their path forward.

I design treatment courses that blend precision imaging, motion management, and supportive care—helping families navigate decisions with clarity and confidence.

My research explores cardiotoxicity surveillance, repeat lung irradiation, and survivorship outcomes, with regular ASTRO talks translating data into everyday practice.
